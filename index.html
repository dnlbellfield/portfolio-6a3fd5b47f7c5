About Me

Hi, I'm FAITH — Architectural Visualizations based in Kenya.

Faith Vision Homes creates cinematic, photo-real architectural visuals for high-end residential developers across Kenya.

We don’t just render buildings. We engineer visuals that de-risk projects and accelerate off-plan sales. Our clients use our work in boardrooms, sales suites, and marketing campaigns to secure funding and close buyers before breaking ground.

Specialization: Bespoke residential visualization from concept to completion.
Our Signature Process: CAD Interpretation → Precision 3D Modeling → Material Curation → Cinematic Lighting Design → Art-Directed Delivery.

Based in Nairobi.
